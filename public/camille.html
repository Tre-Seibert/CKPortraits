LET'S TALK

How do I book a photo session with you?

Whether you want to book with me, Kaili, or you'd prefer both of us to mix up the style, you can click 'Book Now' located on this website and it will direct you to fill out a few questions that help us make sure we know what you are looking for in your session. We love to hear new and unique ideas, get creative with it!

How long does it take to receive the final photos?

Sneak peeks will be delivered within 3 days, while your full gallery will be delivered within 7-14 business days.

How will I access my photos?

I will send you a link that will give you access to your gallery. You can download the pictures right to your phone, computer, etc. It's that simple! As always, if you have any questions feel free to reach out to me.

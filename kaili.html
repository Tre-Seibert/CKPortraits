The Experience

Hi I'm Kaili!
I am a full-time employee, college student, a photographer and my favorite... a dog mom.
I started photography 07/2023 and it has become my favorite hobby.
Yup you read that right. Capturing other people's memories is hands down one of the best feelings in the world.
From family portraits to events to solo shoots I can do it all.

LET'S TALK

How to Book with Us?

Whether you would like to book with myself, Camille, or, the both of us, go to the services page, fill out the quenstionaire and we'll get back to you to learn more abour your ideal session!

What Should I Wear?

Dressing yourself in the morning is hard enough when you don't have to stand in front of a camera and be yourself. My biggest piece of advice to everyone is BE YOURSELF, BE COMFORTABLE. If youre a jeans and sweater kinda person lets lead with that! If youre having any troubles message me maybe I can help!

How Long Until I See my Album?

Typically, your whole session will be ready within 1-2 weeks depending on your package selection. I always am so excited to see how they turned out just as I know you guys are, that being said I will 100% be sending teasers within the 1-2 week time frame.
